StudentMitra


-- Unlock the doors of imagination

StudentMitra is an organization started to help students in developing their skills and exposing their talents and acts as a platform for over-all development of a student. We help students in many ways by conducting sessions, seminars, workshops,lectures etc. and also cultural programmes to let every student skill known to others. It has been six years since StudentMitra started its journey. In this journey, we conducted number of events with great respones from students of different colleges from different states. As our name indicates, we the team StudentMitra acts as a friend to all the students and help them in all ways by conducting cultural events, personality development sessions by famous personalities and speakers. Unlock the doors of imagination is our main motto. We focus on making students more creative, unique, and let their hidden skills and talents shown to the world. We concentrate on areas that students need at a particular time and conduct events in correspondence with the requirement.
